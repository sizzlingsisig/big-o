# Enemy Effect Suggestions

## Stack Overflow

### Option 1: Recursive Clone Explosion (Recommended)
- Spawns smaller "recursive" copies of itself that scatter and deal damage
- Each clone has less HP but can stack up quickly
- Visual: Like a bomb that splits into smaller bombs

### Option 2: Stack Push Knockback
- Pushes the player upward like a stack overflow pushing memory up
- Creates a zone that pushes any player in it upward continuously
- Visual: Purple energy column extending upward

### Option 3: Frame Lag Stun
- Causes the game to "lag" temporarily, freezing player input for 0.5-1s
- Creates a chaotic glitch effect on screen
- Visual: Screen shake + chromatic aberration

---

## Memory Leak

### Option 1: Leaking Puddle Zone (Recommended)
- Leaves a growing purple puddle that drains RAM when player walks through it
- Persists on the ground for several seconds
- Visual: Expanding dark purple circle on floor

### Option 2: Memory Drain Beam
- Fires a continuous beam at the player when in range
- Drains RAM rapidly while beam connects
- Visual: Purple laser beam connecting enemy to player

### Option 3: Fragmenting Projectiles
- Periodically spawns small "fragment" projectiles that orbit the enemy
- Fragments deal small damage on contact
- Visual: Small floating orbs circling the enemy

## Infinite Loop

### Option 1: Time Dilation Zone (Recommended)
- Slows time in an area around the enemy (player moves at 50% speed)
- Creates strategic opportunities to kite
- Visual: Circular zone with clock/timer aesthetic

### Option 2: Repeat Strike
- Repeats its attack pattern 3x in quick succession
- Player must recognize pattern and dodge all three
- Visual: Afterimage trails during attacks

### Option 3: Processor Overload
- Drains player energy/special ability cooldowns
- Prevents use of special moves
- Visual: Red static particles flowing toward player

---

## Null Pointer

### Option 1: Void Zone (Recommended)
- Creates a circular void area that pulls player toward center
- Player takes damage while inside the zone
- Visual: Dark swirling portal on the ground

### Option 2: Dereference Glitch
- Randomly teleports player to random location
- Disorienting but manageable
- Visual: Player blinks out and in with static effect

### Option 3: Nullify Shield
- Becomes invulnerable temporarily and reflects projectile damage back
- Forces player to dodge rather than attack
- Visual: Blue shield envelope around enemy

---

## Heisenberg

### Option 1: Quantum Teleport Strike (Recommended)
- Teleports behind the player and strikes from unexpected angles
- Creates confusion with random teleportation
- Visual: Purple shimmer effect at teleport locations

### Option 2: Entanglement Copy
- Creates a non-physical "clone" that mirrors player's movements
- Both player and clone take damage when they touch
- Visual: Ghostly outline following player

### Option 3: Unstable Explosion
- Becomes unstable and explodes if player gets too close
- High damage radius, forces player to keep distance
- Visual: Pulsing glow that intensifies over time
